Background and CV

I am a registered Professional Geoscientist in the State of Texas with specialization in groundwater, groundwater geochemistry and microbial processes in the subsurface.

I am a professor of geosciences at the University of Texas at Austin, and received a PhD from Syracuse University in geology in 1989, and a MS in Environmental Studies from SUNY in 1986.

I am an author on more than 70 peer-reviewed publications and have over 100 abstracts and meeting presentations.

I have been doing research and litigation support for more than 30 years.
